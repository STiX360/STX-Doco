Media Management

This page outlines the key media management tools we use in our household for organising and acquire local, digital media. Each tool serves a specific purpose, and together they create help us grow and curate our media library.

Radarr​

Purpose:

  • Radarr is a movie collection manager.

Features:

  • Automatically downloads movies based on specific criteria.
  • Monitors our collection for missing or upgraded content.
  • Integrates with Plex or other media servers for easy access.

How We Use It:

  • Set up search criteria for movies we want to watch.
  • Monitor release dates and automate downloads.

Sonarr​

Purpose:

  • Sonarr is a TV series collection manager.

Features:

  • Tracks episodes for TV series you follow.
  • Automates downloading, organizing, and managing TV content.
  • Handles different file qualities and versions.

How We Use It:

  • Automatically download new episodes of ongoing series.
  • Manage our collection of completed TV shows.

Whisparr​

Purpose:

  • Whisparr is used to manage adult or niche video content.

Features:

  • Similar to Radarr and Sonarr but focused on adult video libraries.
  • Automates searching and downloading of specific content types.

How We Use It:

  • Organise and maintain a separate library for specific video collections.

Jackett​

Purpose:

  • Jackett acts as a proxy server that connects Radarr, Sonarr, and other tools to multiple torrent and indexer sites.

Features:

  • Expands the sources available for downloading.
  • Converts search queries into usable results for other tools.

How We Use It:

  • Integrate with Radarr and Sonarr to increase availability of movies and TV shows.

Lidarr​

Purpose:

  • Lidarr is a music collection manager.

Features:

  • Tracks albums and artists.
  • Automates downloading and organizing music libraries.
  • Monitors for album upgrades or missing tracks.

How We Use It:

  • Build a collection of albums and songs from favorite artists.
  • Keep the music library up-to-date.

Readarr​

Purpose:

  • Readarr is used for managing book collections.

Features:

  • Automates downloading of eBooks and audiobooks.
  • Tracks authors and series you follow.
  • Organises books by metadata.

How We Use It:

  • Track new releases from favorite authors.
  • Organise and maintain a digital library of books.

Notes and Tips​

  1. Plex Integration: All tools integrate seamlessly with Plex, making it the central hub for accessing our media.
  2. Backup Settings: Ensure all tools are backed up to avoid losing configurations.
  3. Automation: Use automation responsibly to prevent excessive downloads or unwanted content.
  4. Privacy: Always use a VPN when managing or downloading media for added security.
